城口县

Chengkou County is characterized by a north subtropical mountainous climate, featuring mild temperatures, abundant rainfall, and distinct seasons. The county boasts a wealth of agricultural specialties and traditional Chinese medicinal resources, including raw lacquer, tea, walnuts, and chestnuts. Furthermore, Chengkou County holds the designations of a national e-commerce demonstration county for rural areas, a national health county, a sub-county in the revolutionary cultural relics protection and utilization zone, and a national key county for rural revitalization.
城口县属北亚热带山地气候，气候温和，雨量充沛，四季分明。该县拥有丰富的农业特产和中药材资源，包括生漆、茶叶、核桃和栗子。城口县是全国电子商务农村示范县、国家卫生县、革命文物保护利用区副县、全国乡村振兴重点县。
